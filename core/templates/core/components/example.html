Must-Do Experiences

Top Activities in Oahu

Sunrise at Diamond Head

Hike to the summit of this iconic volcanic crater for breathtaking sunrise views over Waikiki and the Pacific Ocean.

Surfing Lessons

Learn to surf where the sport was born, with gentle waves perfect for beginners at Waikiki Beach.

Pearl Harbor Tour

Pay respects at this historic site, including the USS Arizona Memorial and Battleship Missouri.

Polynesian Cultural Center

Experience the rich cultures of Polynesia through authentic performances, exhibits, and a traditional luau.
