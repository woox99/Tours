Cancellation Policy

Need to cancel your reservation?

Your peace of mind is important to us. Since we do not process payments directly, all cancellations must be handled through the individual tour or activity provider you booked with. Each provider has their own cancellation and refund policy, so we recommend reviewing their terms carefully before booking.
